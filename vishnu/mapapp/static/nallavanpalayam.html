nallavanpalayam

Nallavanpalayam is a census town to Tiruvannamalai Urban Amalgamation in Tiruvannamalai District in Tamil Nadu State in India.[1] Nallavanpalayam is 4.1 km distance from the main city Tiruvannamalai and 161 km distance from its state main city Chennai.
