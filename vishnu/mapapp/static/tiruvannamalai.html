tiruvannamalai temple

The Annamalaiyar Temple in Thiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, is a vast, ancient Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva as Annamalaiyar, located at the foot of the Arunachala hill. It is a significant pilgrimage site, famous for the Karthigai Deepam festival and known for its stunning architecture, extensive complex, and historical contributions from various South Indian dynasties.
